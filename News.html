Today marks the culmination of years of hard work, dedication, and perseverance. Finally, a chapter completed, and the adventure begins!

Reminiscing a memorable chapter brimming with development, camaraderie, and limitless opportunity for the ALS graduates. It was a bittersweet moment as the respective institution witnessed the graduates take their final steps across the stage. The dedicated educators of the each institution beam with pride who have had the privilege of guiding and molding these remarkable minds through their ALS journey.

This momentous event was concluded last July 20, 2023 at Davao del Sur Coliseum, Matti, Digos City, Davao del Sur and was attended by Dr. Maricel S. Langahid (ALS Region XI Focal), Lorenzo E. Mendoza, CESO V (Schools Division Supervisor), Janeth G. Veloso, CESO VI (Assistant Schools Division Supervisor), Dr. Eric Tubat (Division ALS Focal), Davao del Sur School heads and ALS Teachers, Hon. Ronnie L. Sarande (Magsaysay Municipal Mayor) and other government officials.
